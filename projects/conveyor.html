Conveyor Belt System

This mechanical design project involved creating a functional conveyor belt system using Vention and AutoCAD. I designed the layout to meet constraints related to motor mounting, weight distribution, and component selection, then created technical drawings for fabrication.

Mechanical Design Focus

  • Designed a modular conveyor system using Vention's online 3D configurator and mechanical part library.
  • Selected appropriate fasteners, shafts, pulleys, and extrusions to meet design specs.
  • Created 2D technical drawings in AutoCAD to detail shaft placement and tolerances.
  • Calculated spacing, torque loads, and support structure to ensure safe operation and stability.
  • Optimized layout for minimal part count and easy motor integration using motor brackets and tensioners.
