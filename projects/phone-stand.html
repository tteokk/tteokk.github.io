3D Printed Phone Stand

A SolidWorks-based design project to create a functional and aesthetic phone stand for an iPhone XR. The design focused on geometric stability, proper tolerances, and printability for FDM 3D printing.

Mechanical Design Focus

  • Designed a two-part phone stand using SolidWorks with locking interlock slots for modular assembly.
  • Considered center of mass and base width to ensure balance in both portrait and landscape orientations.
  • Tested print tolerances and used chamfers and fillets for edge smoothing and comfort.
  • Incorporated support-free angles to optimize for 3D printability on a single-extruder printer.
  • Printed on PLA material using an FDM printer and confirmed stability with phone in use.
